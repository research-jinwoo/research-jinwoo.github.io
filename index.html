Research

I continously seek to work on imitating the human brain more and more. This is, I work with generative AI models with different types of inputs that range from, but not limited to, text, images, and actions. Specifically, I currently work with multimodal models to have machine learning models improve from single modality to multi modality to imitate the human brain's thought-to-action process.
