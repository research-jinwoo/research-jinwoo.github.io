Jinwoo Ahn

My name is Jinwoo Ahn. I am a third year undergraduate student at Berkeley EECS.

I am affiliated with Berkeley AI Research advised by Roei Herzig and Professor Trevor Darrell with a focus on multimodal language models.

I am also a student researcher at Boeing Research & Technology working on generative computer vision.

Previously, I worked with Ruiqi Zhong and Professor Jacob Steinhardt on concrete natural language processing tasks.

Research

I continously seek to work on imitating the human brain. This is, I work with generative models with different types of inputs that range from, but not limited to, text, images, and actions. Currently, I work with multimodal models to have machine learning models improve from single modality to multi modality to imitate the human brain's thought-to-action process.
